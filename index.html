How to Plan Your Schedule

Freshmen and Sophomores: My biggest recommendation is to take an extra class right now. It might feel like more work today, but you are buying your future freedom. If you stack your credits early, you secure a much lighter 6-class schedule during your junior year.

Juniors and Seniors: By the time you hit your senior year, if you followed this plan, you can drop your schedule down to just 3 or 4 classes total. This gives you the entire afternoon to work, sleep, or focus on your own projects while everyone else is stuck in a classroom.
